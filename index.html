This website calculates the Coordinated Effects Index based on information on firm market shares and one firm’s margin. Enter market shares (0 to 100 and adding up to 100) and one firm’s margin (1 to 100) in the table below. Then click the Submit button to see calculations.

The PIP assumes the absence of buyer power, and uses the alpha parameters to calculate the probability of a price increase. It is analagous to the GUPPI.

The OCE is the level of cost efficiencies such that PIP = 5%.

For more information, see Loertscher and Marx (2021)
